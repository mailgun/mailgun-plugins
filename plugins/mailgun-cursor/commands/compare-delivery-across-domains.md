# Compare Delivery Across Domains

Ask for the domain list, region, date range, and comparison goal.

Use consistent analytics queries across all domains without changing account data. Compare delivery rate, failure rate, complaint rate, unsubscribe rate, open/click engagement, provider/device/country splits, and suppression impact when available.

Return a ranked summary, a metrics table, caveats about unequal configuration, and recommended next steps.
